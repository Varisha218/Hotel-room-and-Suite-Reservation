SINGLE SUITE


The suite includes a private bedroom with a comfortable bed, usually a king-size or queen-size bed. There is a separate living area with seating arrangements, a sofa, and sometimes a coffee table. The suite usually has an en-suite bathroom with standard amenities. It may include a bathtub, shower, and essential toiletries. Many one-bedroom suites provide a dedicated work desk with a chair, catering to business travelers or guests who need a workspace. The suite often comes equipped with a television in both the bedroom and the living area. Some suites may have additional entertainment options. a one-bedroom suite may have a kitchenette equipped with a microwave, refrigerator, and basic kitchenware. In some cases, there might be a full kitchen. High-speed internet access is typically available in the suite, allowing guests to stay connected. one-bedroom suites may offer additional amenities such as complimentary snacks, a minibar, and access to fitness facilities. The suite may provide views of the surrounding area, though this can vary based on the hotel's location and design.

Price: $500 per night



DOUBLE SUITE


The suite includes a bedroom with a double bed, providing accommodation for two people. There may be a separate living area with seating, a sofa, and possibly a coffee table. The suite typically comes with a private bathroom that includes standard amenities, such as a shower or bathtub. Some double suites may feature a work desk with a chair, catering to business travelers or guests who need a workspace. A television is often provided in both the bedroom and the living area. Certain double suites may have a kitchenette with a microwave, refrigerator, and basic kitchenware. In some cases, there might be a full kitchen. High-speed internet access is usually available in the suite to keep guests connected. double suites may offer extras like complimentary snacks, a minibar, and access to fitness facilities. The suite may provide views of the surrounding area, though this can vary based on the hotel's location and design.

Price: $550 per night
